SUBJECT: Artho-M-026
CURRENT LOCATION: Earth, Nizhny Novgorod sector.
STATUS: Adaptation (84%)

FIRST CONTACT:
After the quantum jump, Artho's systems rebooted in a dense forest a few kilometers from the city. The lack of heavy armor saved him from detection by military radars but made the landing process painful. For the first two days, nanites were restoring the damaged visor sensors while Artho hid in an abandoned industrial hangar.

ADAPTATION:
Instead of hiding, Artho chose a "digital mimicry" strategy. He connected to the local Wi-Fi network and, in just a few hours, downloaded gigabytes of data about human culture, language, and memes. Nizhny Novgorod attracted him with its eclecticism — a mix of old architecture and high technology.

INTERESTING OBSERVATIONS:
- Local "organics" (especially shawarma from Srednoy) turned out to be a surprisingly effective fuel for his bio-reactor.
- He is often mistaken for a very advanced cosplayer, which allows him to move freely around the city.
- Artho discovered a strange attachment to the view of the confluence of two rivers (the Oka and the Volga) — his sensors record an anomalous state of calm at this point.

CURRENT GOAL: Searching for rare electronic components at radio markets to attempt transmitter calibration for "Selena-4".
